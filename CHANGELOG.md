The ~1,500-item escalated backlog drains over the next several runs (bounded by `PER_SOURCE_CAP`). Ingest/triage/OG-enrich/backfill/moment were all healthy; only scoring was affected.

## [2026-06-29] Feat: self-draining topic-tag backfill

## [2026-06-29] Feat: sectioned feed â€” Top Stories, Moment, and Category rails
